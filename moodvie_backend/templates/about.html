ABOUT MOODVIE

MOOVIES FOR YOUR MOOD

Ever sit on the sofa in front of the telly, click on Netflix and 30 minutes later you still haven’t decided on what to watch? Yeah us too, we movie buffs have been plagued with decision fatigue. In the light of the current COVID-19 pandemic, we are spending a considerable time in doors. Therefore, we at Moodvie aim to ease your browsing experience by deciding for you!


After asking a range of cinephile’s some questions we found that 97.4% believed their mood influences their browsing and consumption behaviours. We also found that (%age) browse for new content than a film favourite.


So whether you're in a good mood or not, we categorised some ‘hidden gem’ movies into four feelings for you to enjoy. Want to feel uplifted or experience the love of couples, families and friends? Only got a lunch break? Want to explore the independent film world? Whatever you’re in the MOOD for…
